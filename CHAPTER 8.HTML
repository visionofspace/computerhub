CHAPTER 8 (MOTION)

IMPORTANT DEFINATIONS

MOTION

Change in position of any body is called motion

DISTANCE

Length between two point (place) is called its distance.

SPEED

Rate of covering a distance (Rate of Motion) of a body in a interval of time is called speed

DISPLACEMENT

Shortest distance between two points (place) is called displacement.

VELOCITY

The speed (Rate of Motion) of a body in a fixed direction is called velocity.

average velocity = final velocity (v) - initial velocity (u) / 2

=) v average = v-u / 2

ACCELERATION

Rate of change of velocity is called acceleration.

acceleration = final velocity - initial velocity / time

a = v-u / t
